    Given the equation
    \[F = ma\]

        Start with this.
        \begin{align}
            F           & = ma        & \text{divide by \(a\)} \\
            \frac{F}{a} & = \boxed{m} & \text{Wow}
        \end{align}
        \boxed{This} is the final answer.
    \end{solution}
\end{problem}
